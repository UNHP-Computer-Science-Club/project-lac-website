About Us


We are a group of high school students who are passionate about helping those in need. Thousands of children in our community are not given the opportunity to obtain proper education, either due to financial burdens or simply becuase they don't have resources available to them. This inspired us to essentially give back to the underprivileged youth and spread awareness about this inequality through collective efforts.


Mission Statement

We strive to faciliate greater learning and opportunities for underprivileged children in our community through service-based initiatives!
